Milton Glaser


MediumAdobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign

Year2018

This piece was created as a tribute to renowned American graphic designer Milton Glaser, whose works include the iconic I heart NY logo as well as the psychedelic Bob Dylan poster. Multiple styles commonly used by Glaser are incorporated in this poster, including the grid gradient background and the colorful mosaic-like design of the subject. In the center of the piece is a mosaic-like portrait of Milton Glaser himself and around the portrait are circles which contain some of Glaser's previous works from which the poster is inspired from. The font used in the poster is also one which Glaser uses in some of his previous works.
