Joker


MediumAcrylic paint, mix media paper, glue

Year2018

Dimension11"x14"

The process of creating "Joker" consisted of painting pieces of paper different values of black and white and hand-ripping said paper. The ripped pieces of paper are then formatted and glued down to a larger piece of paper in order to create a black and white image of Christopher Nolan’s Joker.
Monochromatic in color, “Joker” is, inadvertently, reminiscent of the noir era of film. This is strangely appropriate, considering the subject of the work is a physical embodiment of the cynical and criminal elements of film noir. Add to that the general chaotic nature of the piece, the character of Joker is fully represented in this piece.
