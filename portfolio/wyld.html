Lost in Translation


MediumAdobe Photoshop

Year2020

The illustration is created as a tribute to LINE Webtoon "Lost in Translation," created by artist Jjolee, as the webtoon relaunces as a licensed webtoon. The subject of the piece consists of the main character, Ahn Jaewon, wiping off sweat after practicing for the upcoming comeback of his idol group. This premise is in consideration of the fact that the relaunch of the webtoon is being treated as a "comeback" of the fictional idol group, Mayhem, of which Ahn is a part of.
