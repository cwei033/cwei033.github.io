De Anza


MediumBlack charcoal pencil, paper

Year2018

Dimension18"x24"

This illustration is created as a reminiscence of my time as a student at De Anza Community College. The building depicted is of the Art Department Office Building which I often passed by on the way to class. I was mainly inspired by the way in which light traveled through the trees and hit the ground, creating an intricate pattern in the shadows.
