Self


MediumAdobe Photoshop, old cards given by friends and families

Year2020

Dimension1630x1500

The concept of the self is explored through this piece. More specifically, "Self" explores the idea that the people that we meet throughout our lifetimes are what shapes us into who we are today regardless of whether or not we are still in contact with them.
"Self" employs both digital and traditional media in its creation. In terms of the traditional aspect, old cards given by friends and families are arranged in collage form and is captured in photo. The photo is then rendered black and white in order to invoke a sense of nostalgia and masked onto a silhouette of a woman. Finally, old passages from previous journal exchanges with friends are included in order to complete the shape of a square.
