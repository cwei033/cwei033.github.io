Pictionary: Halloween Edition

Medium: Javascript

Year: 2020

Dimension: screen size

Go to Site

With shelter-in-place happening in the midst of the coronavirus pandemic, it is hard for people to celebrate Halloween together with their friends. Thus, this website was created so that for those celebrating alone, they can nonetheless get into the Halloween spirit and interact with friends while still practicing social distancing.
