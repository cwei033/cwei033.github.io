Alice's Castle Escape

Medium: Piskel, Unity

Year: 2020

Dimension: screen size

Go to Game

“It’s tea party day in the Queen of Heart’s castle and Alice is invited! Little does Alice know though, the inhabitants of Wonderland were up to no good! They trapped Alice in the castle and it is up to Alice to find items to help her with her escape!”

Alice’s Castle Escape is an escape room digital game created in collaboration with fellow San Jose State University students Natisha Khadgi (programmer) and Stephanie Vang (accompanying artist). The game itself comprises of three rooms, with myself in charge of drawing the bedroom, foyer, and the arrows and volume icons. Along with the art, I was also responsible for finding the sound elements for the game.
