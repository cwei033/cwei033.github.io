The Cage

Medium: Maya

Year: 2020

Dimension: 1920x1080p

This installation seeks to emulate a marriage of sorts between the gentle and ominous aspects of nature. Containing both delicate and dangerous elements, the installation features a shining bud encompassed by onyx-like trees. The purple lighting lends itself to the dark forest aura of the piece. The main inspiration for the model comes from Alvaro Urbano's exhibition at La Casa Encendida.
