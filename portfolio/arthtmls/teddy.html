Teddy Circus

Medium: Javascript

Year: 2020

Dimension: 400x400p

Go to Site

"Teddy Circus" originates from my desire to create a culmination of my favorite aesthetics as a representation of my so-called avatar. The said aesthetics include a classical circus theme as well as teddy bears with mismatched colorings. The mismatched coloring of the bear is also a slight nod to my occasional tendency to experiment with numerous hair colors, including split hair colors. The avatar includes an interactive "balloon-popping" game, a counter keeping track of the number of balloons popped, a confetti button, and a microphone input controlling the bear's arms. Pressing the bowtie of the bear also causes the button eyes to move.

Note: Google Chrome has a tendency to mess with the microphone input. Please view "Teddy Circus" on a different browser if the input does not work.
