Love & Time

Medium: Javascript

Year: 2020

Dimension: screen size

Go to Site

Through Love and Time, I sought to create a music-based game that merges narrative and sound into one cohesive experience. Due to the fact that the medium of the piece is based in game, there is an element of interactivity which further brings the audience into the narrative space. The choice to focus on music for this project stems from the ability of sound to elicit certain emotions within individuals; the theme of love was chosen for a similar reason. Love is an emotion that individuals can relate to to a certain extent. At the very least, they have some form of opinion about it. As such, it is easier to elicit an emotional response using this topic. Thus, with the subject of love combined with the melodies used, Love and Time seeks to guide the audience through a short journey of emotion based in game.
