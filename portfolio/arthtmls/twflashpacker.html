Taiwan Flashpacker

Medium: Procreate

Year: 2021

Dimension: 9.7x5.8in

Business card commission done for 'Taiwan Flashpacker', a charter tour/chauffeur service based in Taiwan. Client specifications included the incorporation of the four seasons into the design, with specific flowers representing each season. Along with the business card design, I was also commissioned to create a logo, LINE profile background, and Facebook banner all based on the card design.
