Guava Botanics


MediumAdobe Illustrator

Year2018

Dimension2110x2208

The illustration seeks to accurately capture realistic images of a common guava and the flower from which the fruit originates. This is done in order to emulate the structure of a typical botanical illustration. Consequently, the common name of the fruit (common guava) as well as its Latin name (psidium guajava) are both included in the image.
Guava is chosen as the subject choice due to its association with the country of Taiwan, which is where I spent my childhood before immigrating to America. As such, the piece somewhat stems from a feeling of longing for the country.
