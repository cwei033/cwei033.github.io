Seungmin


MediumAdobe Photoshop

Year2020

Dimension2550x3300

The illustration consists of a male of Asian descent smiling and carrying a duffel bag. The character is modeled after Korean singer Kim Seungmin, who is a part of the idol group Stray Kids. The motivation behind the piece stemmed from a simple desire to improve in the area of character illustration. More specifically, the realm of shading clothes and the face.
