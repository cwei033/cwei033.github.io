Parfum Earrings


Medium3D Modeling in Maya

Year2020

Dimension1920x1080

To me, earrings are as much of a fashion statement as clothing will ever be. As such, I thought it would be interesting to 3D print my own earrings; thus is the inception of Parfum Earrings. Regarding the design of the earring, I was mainly inspired by traditional perfume bottles, which I consider elegant and classic.
