Reminiscent of Japanese otome games, "Let's Play a Game" is a net artwork featuring a small game which relies on the audiences' choices to determine the final outcome. The premise of the game entails a non-linear narrative in which "you are a royal from kingdom unknown. trapped in a maze, you must escape. but who's to say which way to go. left or right, no one knows..." In short, the audience must choose the route to which they wish to proceed, answering questions along the way, in order to ultimately get to one of two possible ending pages.
