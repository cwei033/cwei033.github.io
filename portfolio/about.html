About

I am a Taiwanese-American illustrator working in both digital and traditional format. Currently, I am an undergraduate at San Jose State University seeking to acquire a Bachelor of Fine Arts with a concentration in Digital Media Art. My primary medium of choice is Adobe Photoshop, although I am also adept with Maya Software as well as other Adobe products. In terms of traditional media, I dabble in mediums such as charcoal, colored pencils, wire, et cetera. Typically, my works focus on whimsical subjects, i.e. fairytales and fictional characters. Additionally, I also like to work with pop culture related themes.
