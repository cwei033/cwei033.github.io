Lighting Study


MediumWhite charcoal pencil, black charcoal pencil, color paper

Year2018

Dimension19.5"x25.5"

The scene consists of three basic geometric objects, one ceramic rabbit, and one wine glass. The concept of light and shadow are explored through these objects. As a light source shines down on the objects from above and to the right, different forms of light and shadow are depicted. The effect of using different materials can especially be seen through the difference in shadow value between the geometric cube versus the wine glass. As illustrated, the opacity of the cube shadow is much more pronounced when compared to the wine glass shadow.
