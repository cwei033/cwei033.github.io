Sherger&Sbaise


MediumAdobe Photoshop

Year2019

Dimension1798x2912

"Sherger&Sbaise" (sugar&spice) is part of an original storybook of the same name which details the excitement of a rabbit-like creature as it ponders the best method of which to devour the Alice-like main character. The subject of the illustration "Sherger&Sbaise" is the aforementioned Alice-like main character. The text included in the image is hand-written and designed by the artist. The name of the piece originates from the nursery rhyme "What Are Little Boys Made Of?" in which little girls are said to be made out of "sugar and spice and everything nice."
