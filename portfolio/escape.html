Escape


Medium3D Modeling in Maya and edited with Adobe Premiere Pro

Year2020

Dimension1920x1080

Link to video
The narrative behind the animation stems from my tendency to ease my stress by means of "traveling" into a different world, namely, the world of fictional stories. The gem which appears in world two is meant to symbolize such stories, with the green pertaining to the green used in the Webtoon app logo. The use of black and white versus color is inspired mainly in part by Wizard of Oz, in which Kansas was monochrome whereas Oz was colorful. The monochrome scheme is also in keeping with some of my previous works, such as MonoBoard.
