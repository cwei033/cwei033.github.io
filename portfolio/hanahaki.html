Hanahaki Disease


MediumAdobe Photoshop

Year2020

Dimension2550x3300

[Hanahaki Disease (花吐き病 (Japanese)) is a fictional disease in which the victim coughs up flower petals when they suffer from one-sided love.]
The subject of the illustration features Instagram artist @szainx's character Kai and his struggle with Hanahaki Disease. While suffering, he nonetheless seeks to reach out and aquire the happiness he desires in the form of requited love. Amidst Kai, flower petals stemming from his mouth swirl around, enveloping the character in the emotions of being in a one-sided love.
